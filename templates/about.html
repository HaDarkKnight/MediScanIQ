About MediScanIQ

Our Mission

MediScanIQ is dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare through AI-powered lung disease classification, providing rapid and accurate preliminary diagnoses to improve patient outcomes.

Our Team

Developed by Mohammed Aljammal and Abdullah Abu Fodeh, under the supervision of Khalid Alemerien, our team combines expertise in AI and healthcare to deliver a cutting-edge diagnostic platform.

Technology

Our custom Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) achieves 92.98% test accuracy, trained on over 23,000 images. Grad-CAM ensures interpretable results by highlighting key X-ray regions.
