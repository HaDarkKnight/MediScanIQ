Security & Compliance

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At MediScanIQ, we prioritize the security and privacy of your medical data. Our platform employs industry-standard encryption and compliance measures to protect your information.

Data Encryption

All uploaded X-ray images are encrypted using the Fernet symmetric encryption algorithm, ensuring that your data remains secure both in transit and at rest.

Compliance

We adhere to strict data protection regulations, including GDPR and HIPAA, to safeguard your personal and medical information.

Audit Logging

Every action, including uploads and account access, is logged securely to maintain transparency and accountability.

For any questions about our security practices, please contact us.
